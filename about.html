about

Hi!

I'm Courtney Olivia- a web developer and visual artist based in Atlanta, Georgia. I'm also a graduate of the Georgia Tech Full-Stack Web Development course and a senior at the University of Maryland University College with a major in digital media and web technology, and a minor in computer science. My favorite stack right now is React.js on the frontend and Node.js on the backend. My preferred artistic medium includes watercolor and markers. I have experience working with Vue.js, CSS, ReactJS, React Native, Angular, and jQuery.

I have been practicing art for over 10 years and the realization of my love for coding grew while studying Japanese abroad in Tokyo. I figured if I love spoken languages so much, why not give computer languages a chance? I haven't looked back since. The challenges coding brings are truly addictive. I look forward to continuously advancing my skills and incorporating my art into my future web development projects.
